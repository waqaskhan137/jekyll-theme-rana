This is the demo site for **jekyll-theme-rana**, a reading-first Jekyll theme by
[Rana Waqas](https://waqasrana.space), based on [Chirpy](https://github.com/cotes2020/jekyll-theme-chirpy) 7.5.0.

Open any post to see the reading mode: the sidebar and panels disappear, the text sets in
Charter serif at a comfortable measure, and a single arrow takes you back home.

Install and configuration: see the [README](https://github.com/waqaskhan137/jekyll-theme-rana#readme).
